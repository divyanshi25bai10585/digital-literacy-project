## Practical Safety Tips
1. Always use strong passwords and change them regularly; enable two-step verification where possible.  
2. Keep your laptop, phone, and apps updated to protect against malware.  
3. Be cautious of unknown links, pop-ups, and email attachments—they may be phishing attempts.  
4. Never share UPI PINs, OTPs, or bank account details with anyone, even friends.  
5. Double-check payment requests before transferring money online or via UPI.  
6. Limit personal info on social media and review your privacy settings frequently.  
7. Think twice before posting photos, location, or personal updates online.  
8. If you experience cybercrime, report it immediately via the **National Cyber Crime Portal**: [cybercrime.gov.in](https://cybercrime.gov.in) or call **Helpline 1930**.  
9. Use trusted Wi-Fi networks; avoid public Wi-Fi for online payments.  
10. Educate your peers about scams and share safe online habits.
