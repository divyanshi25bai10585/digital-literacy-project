# Responsible Social Media Use Checklist

## Do's
- Do keep your profiles professional, especially on platforms like LinkedIn.
- Do post positive and constructive content that adds value.
- Do check privacy settings regularly to control who sees your posts.
- Do take breaks from social media to avoid burnout.
- Do give proper credit when sharing someone else's content.

## Don'ts
- Don't overshare personal opinions on controversial topics.
- Don't use social media to vent frustration or anger.
- Don't follow or interact with accounts that promote negativity.
- Don't ignore messages or comments from classmates or colleagues.
- Don't assume everything online is true—verify before reacting.
